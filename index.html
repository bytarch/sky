Sky helps with anything it sees or hears.

To get started with Sky, you will need to enter your API key (also referred to as an account key) into the application. You can find your key in the Bytarch dashboard after logging in: bytarch.netlify.app/dashboard.

Sees what you see

Sky sees and understands all the content on your screen, but only starts monitoring if you allow it, providing context-aware assistance.

Hears what you hear

Sky records audio via your microphone only when you click the microphone button, allowing for voice-based interactions.

Answers anything

Just press a key. Sky knows what you're doing and responds with exactly what matters — no switching tabs, no guesswork.

Modes and Shortcuts

Default Mode (Text-Only)

This mode is for general text-based queries to the AI.
Shortcut: Alt+A (Ask AI)

Monitoring Mode (Vision)

This mode allows the AI to analyze your screen content (e.g., images, text on screen) for visual understanding and response. Ideal for real-time assistance based on your desktop activity.
Start Monitoring: Alt+S
Stop Monitoring: Alt+X

Audio Capture Mode

This mode transcribes desktop audio and sends it to the AI for processing, allowing for voice-based interactions.
Toggle Audio Capture: Alt+R

Combined Capture Mode

This mode starts both screen monitoring and audio capture simultaneously, providing the AI with both visual and auditory context.
Start Combined Capture: Alt+M

General Shortcuts

Toggle Window Visibility: Alt+F1
